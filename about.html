Thanks for stopping by!

That's me in the picture. I'm a mechanical engineer with a background in robotics and mechatronics. I may be at the beginning of my career, but I've been building things for a long time and I love getting my hands dirty in the shop, in addition to practicing the more analytic side of the art.

I'm interested in electromechanical systems, rapid prototyping, fluid flow, and learning new things! Right now, I'm learning more about all kinds of fabrication and design techniques under the tutelage of the experienced team at AFT.

In my free time, I like to hike and mountain bike, play the guitar, and build projects that force me to learn something new. Right now, that's learning HTML and CSS by building this website!
